My Shopping List

Enter your shopping list items one by one. To add an item, type it into the field and click the "enter" button (or press the Enter key on your keyboard).

To cross off an item but keep it visible on the list, click on it.
To delete a new item, click the "DELETE" button next to it.

P.S. this tool comes with three essential items pre-loaded! click them to cross them off.
